LEVEL - 1

READING CAPABILITY

Building Strong Foundations:

Start your child's educational journey by mastering reading skills. Help them develop the ability to comprehend and analyze written content, laying a solid foundation for future learning.

LEVEL - 2

VISUALIZING CONCEPTS

Unleashing Creativity:

At this level, your child will learn to convert their understanding into visual representations. They will draw connections and relationships between keywords, creating diagrams that vividly showcase their comprehension.

LEVEL - 3

SPEAKING SKILLS

Captivating Audiences:

Take your child's communication skills to the next level by encouraging dynamic spoken interactions. Help them express ideas fluently and confidently while captivating their audience for a whole minute.

LEVEL - 4

WRITING PROFICIENCY

Expressing Ideas with Precision:

Expressing Ideas with Precision: Once your child has conquered reading, it's time to enhance their writing skills. Support them in articulating their thoughts effectively through clear and concise written communication.
