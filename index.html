WHY ELDC?

In today's education system, traditional learning often falls short in preparing students for real-life challenges. Schools mainly focus on subject knowledge, overlooking crucial life skills like goal-setting, critical thinking, and creativity. This leaves students unprepared for the complexities of the modern world.

Therefore, through ELDC, we strongly advocate for an education approach geared towards fostering essential life skills and cultivating core human values.

OUR INITIATIVE

At SPLBS, we redefine education. We've crafted ELDC- Education and Life-Skills Development Course, where learning goes beyond textbooks, where students are taught not only the knowledge but 'HOW TO LEARN' it. We give students clear goals for different subjects like English, Science, Social Studies, etc., helping them reach the goals and teach them to take charge of their own learning. We're addressing the problem by offering a comprehensive solution that not only equips students with academic knowledge but also empowers them with the practical skills needed for success in all aspects of life.

Our goal is more than just passing tests - it's about making learning fun and helping kids become smart and creative.

ELDC stands for Education and Life Development Skills. It's a comprehensive course designed to empower students with essential skills required for independent learning across various subjects like English, science, social studies, etc.

ELDC addresses a critical flaw in the education system by focusing on fostering independent learning skills. Traditional education often lacks in nurturing these essential skills, whereas ELDC provides techniques and strategies for reading, mind mapping, writing, speaking, and most importantly, motivates students to learn independently.

ELDC tackles the flaw by actively teaching students how to learn independently from text. By providing diverse content from multiple subjects, we equip students with essential skills while nurturing their ability to learn autonomously.

Our motivation system is a cornerstone of ELDC. We've developed a unique approach appreciated by both parents and students. Through tailored motivational techniques, we encourage students to engage actively in their learning process, fostering better behavior, sustained interest, and a positive attitude towards education.

ELDC has been a game changer in the realm of online and offline education. Students have shown remarkable behavioural improvements, demonstrating increased interest and enthusiasm for learning. Our approach has transformed their engagement and motivation levels significantly.

Independent learning skills are crucial for lifelong success. They enable students to become self-sufficient learners, equipped to navigate the complexities of education and adapt to various challenges they'll encounter in their academic and professional lives.

Parents can support their children by encouraging and reinforcing the learning techniques taught in ELDC. Additionally, staying engaged and involved in their child's progress and expressing positive reinforcement for their efforts can further enhance the learning experience.
