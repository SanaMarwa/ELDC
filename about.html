Empowering Students for Success and Beyond

Our mission is to empower students to excel not only in English reading, writing, speaking, and mind mapping but also in the art of effective learning, presentation skills, and creativity. We believe that every student is unique, and our program is tailored to meet the specific needs and aspirations of each individual.

Individual Attention:

We understand the importance of individual attention in the learning process. In our sessions, our experienced instructors focus on each student, ensuring they receive personalised guidance and support. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all education; we tailor our approach to each student's pace and learning style.

Continuous Evaluation:

We implement a robust system of individual evaluation. Regular assessments and feedback sessions are conducted to monitor your child's progress, identify strengths, and address weaknesses. This approach ensures that your child's learning journey is continuously adjusted to maximize their potential.

Enhancing Creativity:

We nurture creativity and critical thinking. Our curriculum incorporates creative activities, fostering a happy environment where students become self motivated and can explore their innovative ideas and effectively word their thoughts.

Building Confidence:

Effective communication is not just about words; it's about confidence. Our program strongly emphasises building self-assurance, self-motivation and helping students express themselves with clarity and conviction.

How We Foster Effective Learning

Structured Curriculum:

Our program offers a well-structured curriculum for each level, ensuring students receive a comprehensive education in English and vital life skills.

Interactive Classes:

Our live classes, both online and offline are designed to facilitate engagement and interaction, allowing students to actively participate, ask questions, and collaborate with their peers.

Hands-on Activities:

Learning by doing is the most effective method. Our program includes hands-on activities and practical exercises that encourage students to apply what they've learned.

Feedback and Improvement:

Regular assessments and individual evaluations provide students with feedback on their performance. We work together with parents and students to set goals and track progress.

Adaptive Learning:

Our remarkable results are the fruits of our adaptive teaching methods to accommodate the unique strengths and weaknesses of each student, ensuring that they receive the guidance and support they need.

Motivation System:

Through tailored motivational techniques, we encourage students to engage actively in their learning process, fostering better behavior, sustained interest, a happy environment and a positive attitude towards education.

What Led the Founders to the Education Industry:

Realizing the shortcomings of the present education system, where memorization takes precedence over effective learning, our founders recognized the crucial need for a shift. Education, they believed, should go beyond textbooks, encompassing essential life skills such as goal-setting, critical thinking, and problem-solving. Fueled by this understanding, our founders ventured into the education industry with a mission to reshape the way children learn. This vision paved the way for the establishment of "SPL (Student's Practical Learning) School."

The Inception of SPLBS (Student's Practical Learning - Business Solutions):

Amidst the challenges of a global pandemic, we witnessed the remarkable success of online teaching, spanning from pre-primary to advanced classes. This, coupled with earnest requests from parents eager to extend our unique teaching approach far and wide, prompted the inception of SPLBS. Our commitment to practical, holistic learning now extends beyond traditional classrooms, reaching students globally through our innovative online platform.
